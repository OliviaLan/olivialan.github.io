In this study, we are interested in how people respond to infographics emotionally.
You will be looking at a set of 20 infographics, displayed one at a time;
and you will be responding your feelings on a 7-point scale
according to the degree to which you feel the specified emotion.
Please note that 1 indicates "not at all" and 7 indicates "a great amount".

Your choice should reflect your immediate personal experience, and no more.
Please score AS YOU ACTUALLY FELT WHILE YOU WATCHED THE INFOGRAPHICS.
After you finish the task, we pay you $0.5.
